The H-Factor System methodically identiﬁes, measures and avoids stocks that erode returns.

We believe Alphabet, the parent company of Google, is unlikely to erode your portfolio’s returns

Thus, according to the H-Factor, there is a 98.1% chance the company will deliver the growth to support the stock price.

Investors think in terms of picking winners, when their goal should be to avoid the losers.

We believe stocks with high H-Factor are more likely to be losers.

Other stocks in Alphabet’s Industry Group have higher H-Factor.

Consider replacing such companies with Alphabet’s stock.
